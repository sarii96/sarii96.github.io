Target Audience

Our Audience

Our target audience is young adults and families who like to do outdoor recreational activities in order to have an
unforgettable time with family and friends.Therefore, we want to provide you with information about the weather
as well as recommendations to have the best possible expericence when you visit Idaho.

Two Personas

David Smith: David is 33 years old and is married with two kids. He likes to go to fishing and hinking.
He used to go to fishing with his father all the time when he was younger, so he would like to teach his kids how fish.

Valerie Jonhson: Valerie is 23 years old and single. She is an active person who likes to have adventures. She loves hiking and rock climbing.
